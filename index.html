Don't be trashy!

Trash your EE* responsibly.

*EE: Electrical & Electronic Waste



Oh, My EE! is an initiative by 2nd year Master's students of Srishti Manipal Institute of Art, Design and Technology to generate awareness about WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) generation & disposal.

Interested in knowing more about the aspirations and the team behind this project? Click here!


Taking apart old electronics means battling suffocating heat and hazardous chemicals with little protection.

- The Wire



Workers disassemble various electronic devices, primarily with their own hands, a hammer, and pliers, in an effort to recover valuable metals like gold, silver, and tin - or any other useful object.


"While working I get minor injuries, but I neglect them. If one of us stops working for a day we won’t be able to make ends meet."

- Aftab, a 15-year old boy
